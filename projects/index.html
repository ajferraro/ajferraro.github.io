Stratospheric aerosol geoengineering. This was the subject of my PhD work but I have kept thinking about geoengineering since.

Stratosphere and climate change. The stratosphere, once thought to be a quiescent layer stuck on top of the troposphere, has been shown to influence climate lower down through dynamical and radiative effects. I am interested in how we can use our knowledge of the stratosphere to understand what we might expect under human-driven global warming.

Water vapour and lapse rate feedbacks. The water vapour feedback is the largest positive feedback in the climate system. I am interested in how confident we are of our theories about it, and also in its regional pattern as a diagnostic of physical processes and a driver of regional climate change.
