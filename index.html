I work in the Exeter Climate Systems group at the University of Exeter. I am interested in a broad range of topics in climate research, mainly focused on the interaction between the atmospheric circulation and radiative transfer. I'm always in hearing from people interested in my research, and climate science in general.

Research interests:

  • Water vapour and cloud radiative feedbacks.
  • Interaction between radiative transfer and large-scale circulation.
  • Stratospheric influence on the climate system.
  • Solar radiation management geoengineering.
